Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

October 1, 2023 by Mickoy Banton

Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is a rapidly advancing field that has been making headlines and reshaping industries in recent years. With its ability to process vast amounts of data, learn from it, and make predictions or decisions.

AI is becoming an integral part of our daily lives. In this blog post, we will delve into the evolution, current state, and the profound impact of artificial intelligence on various aspects of our society.

Artificial Intelligence Evolution

September 27, 2023 by Mickoy Banton

Artificial Intelligence has come a long way since its inception. The roots of AI can be traced back to ancient mythologies, where automatons and intelligent machines were often featured.

However, it wasn't until the 20th century that AI as a formal discipline began to take shape. The early days of AI research focused on symbolic AI, where computers were programmed with explicit rules to mimic human intelligence. Prominent AI pioneer Alan Turing proposed the Turing test in 1950.
